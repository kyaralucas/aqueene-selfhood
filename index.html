About the opening

We invite you to arrive slowly into an evening shaped by rest and selfhood. Please choose a timeslot so the space stays gentle, spacious, and attentive. Timeslots run between 19:00 and 21:00. Please arrive on time for your chosen slot


After your visit, you're warmly welcome to stay. Drinks and snacks are available in the adjacent room, and you can linger there as long as you like. The timed entry only applies to the exhibition space itself.


Directions

Registration is free. Limited capacity per timeslot to keep the experience soft and unrushed.

RESTING SPACE

Before (or after) registering for the opening, step into a small digital resting space. Breath, softness, and a few words to hold.
