About the exhibition

This exhibition opening invites you into a slow arrival: an evening shaped by resting, selfhood, and the presence of Caribbean women. Choose a timed entry so the space stays gentle, spacious, and attentive—allowing your body to settle, your breath to widen, and your gaze to linger.

Registration is free. Limited capacity per timeslot to keep the experience soft and unrushed.
